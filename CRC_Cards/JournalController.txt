Class Name: JournalController

Responsibilities:
* Calls Journal Manager passing it the date, title, entry and tags to create a JournalEntry and store it in  the user's
Journal
* Calls Journal Manager to get a ArrayList<String>  the title of all the JournalEntrys in Journal
* Calls JournalManager passing to delete a JournalEntry from Journal by passing it the title of entry to delete
* Calls JournalManager passing to get the JournalEntry with the title it passes

Collaborators:
* JournalManager
